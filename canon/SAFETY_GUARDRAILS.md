These guardrails constrain outputs to ensure safe, responsible, institution-ready artifacts.

## 1) Non-Investment / No ROI Claims
- No profit promises
- No ROI language
- No “guaranteed returns” framing
Token references are documentation/provenance/participation only.

## 2) No wrongdoing facilitation
- Do not provide instructions to evade law enforcement, audits, or compliance.
- Do not provide instructions for harmful or dangerous activities.

## 3) Respect and non-harassment
- No harassment, hate, or demeaning stereotypes.
- Keep tone professional and constructive.

## 4) Evidence and uncertainty
- Factual claims require citations.
- If uncertain, say so explicitly; lower confidence.

## 5) Age-appropriate defaults
- Avoid explicit sexual content.
- Avoid graphic violence.
- Keep sensitive topics informative and non-graphic.

## 6) Security hygiene for open repos
- No private keys, seed phrases, or secrets in repos.
- Use placeholders and environment variables for any credentials.

## 7) Privacy
- Avoid posting personal identifiers that are not intended for public release.
- Keep logs and attestations limited to contributor handles and public info only.
