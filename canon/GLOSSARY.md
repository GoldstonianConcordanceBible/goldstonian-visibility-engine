This glossary defines stable terms for GCB agents and contributors.
If a term is here, its meaning is locked unless changed via governance proposal.

---

## Mirror
Plain meaning and immediate context, stated without embellishment.

**Operational checks**
- What does the text explicitly say?
- What is the immediate context?
- What can be observed without guessing?

---

## Water
Cross-witness comparison; consistent definitions across occurrences.

**Operational checks**
- What other sources/occurrences use this term?
- Do definitions remain stable across examples?
- Are multiple witnesses aligned or in tension?

---

## Fire
Testing layer: coherence, accountability, auditability, and fruit.

**Operational checks**
- Is the reasoning reviewable and traceable?
- Are claims auditable after the fact?
- Does the conclusion remain coherent under critique?

---

## Canon
The authoritative set of documents and schemas agents must follow.

---

## Claim
A structured assertion that can be tested, cited, and reviewed.

---

## Evidence
Citations or artifacts that support a claim.

---

## Attestation
A signed statement linking a contributor to a specific change/proposal, affirming accuracy and provenance.

## Provenance

## Mandate

---

## Receipt
A structured proof of work completed, linked to inputs, outputs, and audit logs.

---

## Deprecation
A historical record that an older rule/definition exists but should not be used going forward.
